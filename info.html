Scientific Information

Tomato Seeds (Solanum lycopersicum)

Tomatoes belong to the nightshade family and are rich in lycopene, a natural antioxidant. They require full sun and moderate watering.

Basil Plant (Ocimum basilicum)

Basil is an aromatic herb known for its essential oils and culinary value. It thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil.

Lavender (Lavandula angustifolia)

Lavender is drought-tolerant and used for its calming scent. It prefers sandy soil and plenty of sunlight.
